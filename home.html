welcome TO WOLDIYA UNIVERSITY

Woldia University is a public university located in Woldia, Ethiopia. This institution of higher learning was established in 2011, though its foundations were laid a few years prior. In 2008, key figures in the regional government and education ministry officiated the groundbreaking ceremony, marking the start of construction. The university was designed to serve as a hub for academic excellence and development in the region, providing educational opportunities to students in various disciplines. The university opened its doors to its first class of students in December 2011, welcoming nearly 600 individuals into four faculties and twelve departments. This inaugural ceremony was a significant event, attended by local residents, government officials, and members of the university community. The university is also committed to contributing to the development of the region and the country by producing graduates equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to address local and global change.
